Bangalore

Bangalore has been Ranked most Dynamic city in the world. Bangalore called as Silicon Valle of India, it has has highest number of IT companies in India. Bangalore also called as Garden city of India.

History

Kempegowda I was the founder of Bangalore in 1537 AD. Early name was Bendha Kaalooru [Bendha Kaalu means Boiled Beans in Kannda] [ooru means City]. Later it became Bengaluru in Kannada, Bangalore in English.
